Document Requirements

  • High school diploma or expected graduation certificate
  • Official transcripts
  • Language proficiency certificates (TOEFL/IELTS)
  • Non-criminal record report

Additional Requirements

  • Foreigner Physical Examination Form
  • Bank Statement (minimum CNY 20,000)
  • Proof of academic achievements (optional)
  • Payment receipt of application fee
